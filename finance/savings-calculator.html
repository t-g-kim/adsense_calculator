Compound interest is when you earn interest on both your initial deposit and the interest that accumulates over time. The longer you save, the more your money grows!

  • Initial Deposit: Your starting amount
  • Monthly Deposits: Regular contributions
  • Interest Rate: Annual percentage yield (APY)
  • Time: Number of years to save
